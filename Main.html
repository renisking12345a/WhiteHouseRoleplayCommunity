Server Rules

Follow Roblox Term of Use.
            Click below to see Roblox ToS.
            https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004647846-Roblox-Terms-of-Use
            
            Random Death Match. (RDM)
            Do not damage other players without any probable cause.
            
            Vehicle Death Match. (VDM)
            Do not ram your vehicle into other players.
            
            Cuff rushing.
            You must not use any non-lethal weapon on a suspect that is holding a weapon. In addition, you must type cuffs before cuffing a player.
            
            Safe-zones.
            All spawn points are safe-zones, meaning you cannot roleplay in them or damage any other players. Spawning vehicles mid-pursuit is also prohibited.
            
            Cop baiting.
            You must not bait police by being too desperate for attention.
            
            Trolling.
            Trolling is completely prohibited as it can lead to a temporary or permanent ban.
            
            New Life Rule. (NLR)
            Once you die at a scene and you respawn, you mustn't return to the same scene.
            
            Special Roleplays.
            Special role-plays such as road-blocks, detours, borders, etc.. must be accepted by staff.
            
            Peacetime.
            During peacetime, you may not trigger any calls or priorities which disturbs the peace. Meanwhile, you may not start any pursuits, shootouts, crashes, etc...
            
            Realistic avatars.
            You must have a realistic avatar in-game.
            
            Disturbing mod scenes.
            Do not engage in moderation scenes that you are not part of.
            
            Thank you for understanding !
